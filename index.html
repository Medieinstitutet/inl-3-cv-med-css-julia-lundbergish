About me

My name is Julia. I'm a digital designer and aspiring developer currently based in Västerås with a passion for creating cool things and solving user issues. I have a Bachelor of Science in Informatics from Högskolan Kristianstad where I studied digital design. During my studies I learned about UX and UI, graphic design, interaction design, web design with front-end development and AR-design. I also completed a design internship at Oh My in Skellefteå during this time. I’m currently a student at Medieinstitutet where I am studying to become a front end developer.
The cross-section between art, humanity and technology is what makes my heart flutter. What about you?
